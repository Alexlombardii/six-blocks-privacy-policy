Privacy Policy for Six Blocks

Last Updated: November 10, 2025

Introduction

Six Blocks ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Six Blocks mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (via Supabase authentication)
  • User ID (automatically generated)

Game Data

  • Current level
  • Token balance
  • Owned themes and powerups
  • Game progress and statistics

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Anonymous advertising identifiers (for AdMob)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the game service
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Track game progress across devices
  • Display personalized advertisements
  • Improve game features and user experience

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

Supabase

  • Provides authentication and database services
  • Stores game progress and user data
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Google AdMob

  • Displays advertisements in the app
  • Collects device and usage data for ad personalization
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Apple App Store

  • Processes in-app purchases
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Children's Privacy

Our service is available to users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.
